The devices used in the project:-

1-GPS Device:-

GPS is a system of positioning, navigation and timekeeping AD and stands for GPS abbreviation (System Positioning Global The) was designed and managed by the US Department of Defense and is presented This system has many advantages that made it the basic technology around the world in collecting spatial data.

2- level device:-

-The scale is a surveying device that works on an engineering drawing of a specific location in order to obtain a horizontal line of sight that is parallel to the scalemean sea level.

Components of the balance device:-

1- A telescopic viewfinder to see the distant points clearly and has two screws to stop its horizontal circular motion. and the other to set its slow motion

2- Inside the telescope, there is an eye lens and another at its end, and inevitably the olfactory lenses

3- The base of the device

4- The axis of rotation, which is the vertical axis.

5- Two leveling scales, one longitudinal and the other circular.

6- A reflective mirror above the longitudinal level to reflect the image of the bubble when it is adjusted during monitoring

3-Total Station Device:-

-The Station Total is one of the most used, integrated and accurate surveying devices at the present time. The name of the device indicates that it includes a number of devices and capabilities within an integrated framework in one device.

Components of the total station:-

1- A digital theodolite.

2 - DEM electronic distance measuring device.

3- An electronic memory to record the measurements.

4- A processor-micro computer unit to run computational programs.

5 - Accessories such as battery, inverter set, tripod and computer connection cable.
